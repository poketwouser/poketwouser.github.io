Engineering intelligenceinto decisions.

I'm a B.Tech student in Artificial Intelligence & Data Analytics at IIT Madras, drawn to problems where mathematics, machine learning, data and engineering intersect — markets, physiology, and large-scale systems.

My work spans quantitative finance (forecasting, portfolio optimization, risk modeling), deep learning & computer vision, agentic AI and data-intensive systems. I care about pipelines that are rigorous and reproducible — leakage-free walk-forward validation over backtest theatre, shrinkage estimators over fragile covariances.
